We are excited to launch a significant update of the Bull Bitcoin integration in the Bitcoin Jungle app, which lets you convert Bitcoin to Colones, send and receive bank payments via Sinpe or IBAN transfer, and allows you to buy Bitcoin using Sinpe or IBAN transfer.

To continue using SINPE or IBAN transfers to buy and sell Bitcoin, you must update your app to the latest version.

Visit the App Store on your device to continue.

Important Differences

1.) The user interface will change and will not look the same. This is normal!

2.) You now have to register for a Bull Bitcoin account using an email and password.

3.) You can create SINPE payment contacts that you can reuse.

4.) You can view your transaction history.
